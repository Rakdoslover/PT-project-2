Squat

With the bar positioned firmly and safely on your neck muscle, put your feet shoulder width apart pointing forwards.

Your hands should be positioned as tight as possible, when your shoulder blades are being squeezed you should be good to go.

Push your hips backwards while bending your knees in a controlled movement downward.

Try to go as deep as possible before turning the movement, never stop fully at the bottom.

Finish the movement standing while clenching your gluteus muscle.

Tip:

While going downward inhale as much air as possible and make your torso into a ballon, this will build-up a pressure around your core making the exercise more stable and safe.
