Deadlift

Start off by standing shoulder width apart with your shins just barely gracing the bar.

Lean forward with straight arms until you can grab the bar with an overhand grip.

Lower your hips as low as you can while still having straight shins or until your upper body is angled vertically or just slightly upwards.

Push off the ground by pushing forward through the hip, end the motion when standing up.

Tip:

Try to puff up your chest and aim it towards the ceiling.
