Hip-Thruster

Place yourself sitting just in front of the box/bench, place your feet shoulder width apart just in front of you and push yourself up on the box so that your shoulder blades are firmly on the box/bench.

Place the bar, preferably equipped with a neck pillow, right over your hip bones.

From the sitting position, push your hips upward while simultaneously leaning backwards onto the box/bench.

At the very top your body should form the “L-shape”, clench your glutes.

Slowly return to the sitting position and repeat.

Tip:

While moving upward, try to push your knees slightly outward. You can also use a bootyband placed just below the knees to make the exercise tougher.
