Calf-Raise

This exercise can be performed either with your feet on a step-board, a box or on the floor. We’ll be using the step-board for these instructions.

Place the board against a wall or somewhere stable, place your feet on the edge of the board a decimeter from each other.

Usually we use a barbell or a dumbbell as a weight but if you're not used to balancing at the edge of the board then start off with just your own body-weight.

Lower your heels towards the ground in a controlled motion. try to stretch your calf muscle as far as possible.

When at the bottom, shoot upward until you’re almost at the tip of your toes.

Tip:

If you want to make the exercise a little tougher try to do one foot at the time, and/or add weights.
